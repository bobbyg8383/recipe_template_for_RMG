This eggplant, tomato, and mozzarella melt can also be used a pasta sauce. To make this recipe a pasta dish, simply add 16 ounces of pasta to the water that you used to boil the tomatoes - add the pasta immediately after finishing Step 4 (blanching the tomatoes). When the pasta is done (usually 10-12 minutes, depending on the pasta) drain it. Just the before topping the eggplant-tomato mix with mozzarella rounds in Step 6 stir the pasta into the pan.
